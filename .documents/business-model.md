# AI Financial Planner Business Model

**Last updated:** 2026-03-18

## Summary
AI Financial Planner is a paid web app for small business owners who need a real 3-year financial plan without advanced spreadsheet skills. The product combines a deterministic finance engine with AI-guided onboarding, explanation, and scenario support.

## Who It Serves
- Primary audience: general small business owners.
- Secondary audience later: operators, advisors, or finance-adjacent helpers working with the owner.

## Core Value
- Turn a complex workbook-driven planning process into a guided web experience.
- Help users answer viability, cash need, break-even, payroll affordability, and growth-scenario questions.
- Keep outputs trustworthy by grounding them in a deterministic model instead of AI-only advice.

## Product Promises
- AI-guided onboarding wizard.
- Editable 36-month financial model.
- Business-type-aware guidance and estimation help.
- Reporting, diagnostics, and scenario comparison.
- PDF and CSV exports.

## Pricing Direction
- Paid plans from day one.
- Launch pricing from the locked PRD:
  - Builder plan: $50
  - Pro plan: $200
- AI usage is included but limited by plan rather than unlimited.

## Delivery Constraints
- US-only launch.
- English-only UI.
- Single-user accounts in v1.
- Spreadsheet upload from arbitrary files is out of scope.
- AI must propose changes before the user approves them.
